Sales Actual Costs and Margins

calculate real margins on sale orders

Define extra costs per product to determine the actual price. The actual price is used to calculate margins on sale orders.

Define Extra Costs for Products

Define various types of extra costs you can add to a product. A couple are predefined, but you can easily adjust these ones. For each type of extra cost you can define a default cost.

Add one or more extra costs to a product. The actual price is automatically calculated and used to determine margins.

View Margins on Sale Orders

On the Sale Order form you can now view the total actual costs on sale orders. Every sale order line and sale order shows the total.

Generate a wide variety of margin reports

Now you are able to view a many different margins reports and filter or group by product categories, partner, sales channel, period, etc. And allows you to make important business decisions easily.

Important remarks

  • To calculate margins on Account Invoices also install the account_margin_actual_cost module.
  • This module is meant to replace the sale_margin module provided by Odoo S.A.
