Accounting Invoice Tax Tests

verify tax bookings of invoices

Various extra tests will we added to the standard Odoo account_test module which check for differences in tax used on account invoices and tax booked in the account move lines.

Generate test report

Generate a PDF report of all customer and supplier invoices with a difference in total tax booked on the invoice, the invoice tax lines and/or the account move lines. If you have inconsistencies in your tax declaration this is the way to find the invoices with problems.
